Get To Know SpongeBob!

A sponge and a snail living in the depth of the ocean.
What could possibly go wrong?
Bob is a sponge that lives in an underwater city known as bikini bottom. He always wears square pants because of his square body. Accompanied by his snail and many friends, SpongeBob leads a happy life working in the Krusty Crab on a daily basis as the chef. His cooking skills are remarkable amongst the sea creatures from all over the ocean.
SpongeBob sometimes encounters difficulties or problems (mostly because of Squidward)
but he is always able to fight through
and spread positivity like the
heroic sponge he is.
